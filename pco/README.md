# Penthouse Compliance & Ops (PCO)

PCO is the future back-office and compliance product for the Penthouse ecosystem.

It is intentionally separate from the live Penthouse Dispatch app for now.

## Purpose

PCO replaces the administrative and compliance-heavy functions that providers often keep in systems like Sentry:

- driver compliance
- vehicle compliance
- company documents
- complaints and incidents
- billing and reconciliation
- driver payout operations
- audit trails

## Relationship to Penthouse Dispatch

- Penthouse Dispatch remains the live dispatch and driver/rider operations app.
- PCO becomes the compliance and back-office app later.
- For now, PCO is scaffolded only. It is not deployed, linked in routes, or exposed to users.

## Phase 1 priorities

1. Drivers and credential review
2. Vehicles and compliance status
3. Company documents and profile
4. Billing and reconciliation
5. Driver pay operations
