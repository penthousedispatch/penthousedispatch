# PCO Workflows

## Driver onboarding review

1. Driver submits required items
2. Company reviews missing fields and documents
3. Reviewer approves, rejects, or requests correction

## Credential expiry

1. Detect expiring document
2. Alert company
3. Optionally block dispatch eligibility

## Vehicle approval

1. Upload registration, insurance, and inspection
2. Validate dates and completeness
3. Approve vehicle for dispatch use

## Complaint resolution

1. Create complaint or incident
2. Assign owner
3. Track notes and follow-up
4. Close with resolution

## Reconciliation

1. Import payout data
2. Compare to completed trips
3. Flag mismatches
4. Approve batch

## Driver payout

1. Select eligible earnings
2. Apply company pay rules
3. Send ACH/manual payout
4. Track failures and retries
